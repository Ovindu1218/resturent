Our Story

The Passion Behind KOCHCHI

KOCHCHI began as a humble family endeavor, born from a deep love for Sri Lankan culinary traditions. Our founder, Chef Anura, grew up learning ancient recipes from his grandmother in the coastal town of Galle.

What started as a small food stall has blossomed into Polonnaruwa's premier destination for authentic Sri Lankan cuisine. Every dish tells a story – of heritage, of family, and of the rich tapestry of flavors that make Sri Lankan food so unique.
